Emphasizing Text : Redefining <em> & <i> elements

Verbal Stress → <em> : My name is Jane, not John.

Alternate Voice → <i> : Sally thought to herself, when will IE6 die?

Adding Importance to Text : Redefining <strong> & <b> elements

Important Text → <strong> : Registration is required for this event.

Stylistically Offset Text → <b> : Once upon a time, there was a man who lost his mind.
